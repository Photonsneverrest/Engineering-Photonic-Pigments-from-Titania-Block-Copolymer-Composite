# Figure 5

This folder contains the data associated with Figure 5 of the manuscript:

"Engineering Photonic Pigments from Titania-Block Copolymer Composites"

## Figure panels

- Fig. 5a: Mean reflectance spectra of four sample batches. Shaded areas represent the standard deviation calculated from multiple individual spheres within each batch.
- Fig. 5b: Light microscopy image of PS-b-P2VP (240-b-296 kDa) with f(TiO2) = 40 %.
- Fig. 5c: Light microscopy image of PS-b-P2VP (320-b-398 kDa) with f(TiO2) = 40 %.
- Fig. 5d: Light microscopy image of PS-b-P2VP (240-b-296 kDa) with f(TiO2) = 0 %.
- Fig. 5e: Light microscopy image of PS-b-P2VP (320-b-398 kDa) with f(TiO2) = 0 %.

## Files

### Reflectance spectra shown in Fig. 5a

- `Fig5a_reflectance_spectra_240_296_TiO2_0%_[i].txt`
- `Fig5a_reflectance_spectra_240_296_TiO2_40%_[i].txt`
- `Fig5a_reflectance_spectra_320_398_TiO2_0%_[i].txt`
- `Fig5a_reflectance_spectra_320_398_TiO2_40%_[i].txt`
with [i] ranging from 0 to 9

These files contain the individual reflectance spectra forming the mean and standard deviation curves in Fig. 5a.

### Light microscopy images shown in Fig. 5b-e

- `Fig5b_light_microscope_raw.tif`
- `Fig5c_light_microscope_raw.tif`
- `Fig5d_light_microscope_raw.tif`
- `Fig5e_light_microscope_raw.tif`

These files contain the light microscopy images corresponding to the image panels in Fig. 5b-e.

## Notes

Image brightness, contrast, cropping, and scale-bar annotations may differ between the raw image files and the final figure panels shown in the manuscript.

The shaded areas in Fig. 5a correspond to the standard deviation of reflectance spectra measured from multiple individual spheres within each sample batch.
