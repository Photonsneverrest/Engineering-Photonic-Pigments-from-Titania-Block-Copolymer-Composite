# Figure 4

This folder contains the raw data associated with Figure 4 of the manuscript:

"Engineering Photonic Pigments from Titania-Block Copolymer Composites"

## Figure panels

- Fig. 4a: Cross-sectional TEM image of PS-P2VP with 45% nanoparticle mass fraction.
- Fig. 4b: Cross-sectional TEM image of PS-P2VP with 90% nanoparticle mass fraction.
- Fig. 4c: Cross-sectional TEM image of PS-P2VP with 150% nanoparticle mass fraction.
- Fig. 4d: Cross-sectional TEM image of PS-P2VP with 45% nanoparticle mass fraction.
- Fig. 4e: Cross-sectional TEM image of PS-P2VP with 90% nanoparticle mass fraction.
- Fig. 4f: Cross-sectional TEM image of PS-P2VP with 150% nanoparticle mass fraction.
- Fig. 4g: Experimentally obtained spectral refractive-index curves for four samples.

## Notes

The computed refractive-index curves shown in Fig. 4g are model-derived values and are not included as separate raw data files. The method used to calculate the computed curves is described in the manuscript. This folder contains the refractive-index curves from a database obtained, underlying Fig. 4g.

Image brightness, contrast, cropping, and scale-bar annotations may differ between the raw files and the final figure panels shown in the manuscript.
