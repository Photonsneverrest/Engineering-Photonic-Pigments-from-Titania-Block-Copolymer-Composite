# Figure 2

This folder contains the raw data associated with Figure 2 of the manuscript:

"Engineering Photonic Pigments from Titania-Block Copolymer Composites"

## Figure panels

- Fig. 2a: Cross-sectional SEM image of room temperature sample
- Fig. 2b: Cross-sectional SEM image of heated sample
- Fig. 2c: Light microscopy image of room temperature sample
- Fig. 2d: Light microscopy image of heated sample
- Fig. 2e: Reflectance spectra of room temperature and heated samples

## Files

### Imaging data

- `Fig2a_SEM_raw.tif`  
  Raw SEM image corresponding to Fig. 2a.

- `Fig2b_SEM_raw.tif`  
  Raw SEM image corresponding to Fig. 2b.

- `Fig2c_light_microscope_raw.tif`  
  Raw light microscopy image corresponding to Fig. 2c.

- `Fig2d_light_microscope_raw.tif`  
  Raw light microscopy image corresponding to Fig. 2d.

### Spectral data

- `Fig2e_reflectance_spectra_room_temp.txt` and `Fig2e_reflectance_spectra_heated.txt`  
  Reflectance spectra shown in Fig. 2e.

## Notes

Image brightness, contrast, cropping, and scale-bar annotations may differ between the raw files and the final figure panels shown in the manuscript.
